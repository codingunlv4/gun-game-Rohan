Castle map environment used in this game:

  Folder: assets/models/castle/sketchfab/
  Format: glTF (scene.gltf + scene.bin + textures)
  Source: Sketchfab (photogrammetry castle / rampart environment)
  License: CC Attribution (verify on the original Sketchfab model page)

The map is loaded at runtime by scripts/map_builder.gd as the primary level geometry.
